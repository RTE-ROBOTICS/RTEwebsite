Why partner with us

More than a logo on a robot.

Institutional credibility
Endorsed by TAMUQ STEM
Our program is officially endorsed by the Texas A&M University at Qatar STEM outreach team — the organising body for the Qatar FTC Championship under HBKU.
Authentic visibility
Competing machine
Your logo is on a robot that moves, competes, and gets filmed. Audiences at FIRST events are engineering students, parents, educators, and industry professionals.
Qatar 2030 alignment
STEM investment mandate
Qatar National Vision 2030 places STEM education at its core. Sponsoring Team #25404 is a direct and credible expression of that commitment.
Technical content
Real engineering documentation
Our public build log documents mechanism decisions with FBDs, PIDF data, and load testing — authentic third-party content for hardware sponsors.
