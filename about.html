About RTE Robotics

A competitive student engineering team based in Doha, Qatar, building robots that compete on the world stage.

Mission

Engineering the next generation.

RTE Robotics exists to develop serious engineering skills in young people through the discipline of competitive robotics. We don't just build robots — we build engineers. Every design decision, PIDF tuning session, and match cycle teaches something real.

Affiliated with the Texas A&M University at Qatar STEM program under HBKU, we are part of Qatar's growing STEM ecosystem and compete as part of the global FIRST Tech Challenge network.
