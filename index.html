What we do

Design. Build. Compete.

Each season FIRST releases a new engineering challenge. We have roughly six weeks to design and build a competition robot from scratch — applying mechanical design, control systems, and software disciplines that mirror real industry practice.

Learn more →
Mechanical
Custom robot design
Full CAD from scratch each season. MISUMI SAR3 linear slides, custom 3D-printed brackets, goBILDA structural components.
Software
Control systems + autonomous
Custom PIDF controllers, Pedro Pathing odometry-based path planning, FTCLib architecture.
Competition
Qatar FTC Championship
Competing against the strongest student engineering teams in the region, targeting Worlds advancement.
